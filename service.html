Group Structure

Consult

Market Entry, Investment Facilitation & Business Growth Advisory

Concierge Consult serves as the strategic advisory and business development engine of the Group.

Concierge Consult It specializes in helping foreign investors, multinational corporations, diaspora entrepreneurs, development organizations, and regional businesses successfully establish, operate, invest, and grow within Ghana.

Its services include:

  • Market Entry Advisory
  • Investment Facilitation
  • Business Establishment Support
  • Strategic Partnerships
  • Government & Regulatory Liaison
  • Local Representation
  • Business Development Advisory
  • Business Process Reengineering
  • Market Intelligence
  • Strategic Communications
  • Branding & Positioning Advisory

As the flagship subsidiary, Concierge Consult plays a central role in attracting clients, facilitating investments, developing partnerships, and generating opportunities for the broader Group.

Concierge Properties

Concierge Properties focuses on property development, real estate investment advisory, property management, and strategic real estate solutions.

Services include:

  • Property Development
  • Property Management
  • Real Estate Investment Advisory
  • Property Acquisition Support
  • Facility Management
  • Real Estate Market Intelligence

The company supports both local and international investors seeking opportunities within Ghana’s growing property sector.

Focus areas include:
