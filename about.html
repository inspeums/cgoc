About the group

Concierge Group of Companies is a Ghanaian-owned business growth, investment facilitation, and enterprise development group established to provide integrated advisory, operational, investment, and sector-specific solutions across strategic industries.

The Group was founded on the belief that sustainable business growth and economic transformation require more than capital. They require trusted partnerships, local knowledge, strategic guidance, strong networks, and effective execution.

Concierge Group therefore serves as a bridge between global opportunities and local implementation by supporting investors, businesses, institutions, and entrepreneurs seeking to establish, expand, invest, and operate successfully within Ghana and emerging African markets.

Through its subsidiaries, the Group provides market-entry support, investment facilitation, business development, operational solutions, property services, energy advisory, logistics support, mobility solutions, and social impact initiatives.

Today, Concierge Group is positioning itself to become one of Africa’s most trusted business growth, investment facilitation, and enterprise development groups.
